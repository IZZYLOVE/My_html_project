Our Internship Programme
Our internship programme is opened to fresh graduates of tertiary institutions and individuals who have received training form accredited ICT centers. Potential interns must be individuals with foundational ICT skills in either programming, graphic design and desktop publishing, who have documented idea for an innovative ICT solutions. Through this programme interns are provided with the opportunity to work with our training and software development units to improve their programming skills to be equipped to develop innovative and scalable solutions. Interested applicants should send us their curriculum vitae and statement of expression of interest with an Idea Concept paper to career@m-rinternational.com . For none graduates who have received ICT training from a recognised training center a minimum of secondary school leaving certificate is required to be admitted as an intern.
