WELCOME TO MRSOFT INTERNATIONAL
Our Story and who we are!
MRsoft is the registered trademark of M-R International Nigeria Limited, an indigenous first class information and communication technology company. M-R International was established in 1992 and registered by Nigeria’s Corporate Affairs Commission with the mandate to provide ICT solutions, offer specialized trainings to equip individuals with ICT skills in the execution of entrepreneurial activities and to offer consultancy services to individuals and corporate organisations who transact business in Nigeria and in the international ICT industry. MRsoft operates from its corporate head office, MRsoft Technology Complex, a well-equipped technological hub located in Port Harcourt, the capital city of Nigeria’s oil rich Niger Delta Region.

Since its emergence MRsoft has metamorphosed into an industry brand synonymous with creativity, quality and professionalism. Its bouquet of services includes the design, development and delivery of value added and innovative ICT solutions and trainings. These services MRsoft offers to public and privately owned organisations at competitive prices. Our journey since our emergence has been facilitated by a team of dedicated and adequately trained professionals who are committed to providing secure and effective ICT solutions to emerging challenges. MRsoft is an environmental friendly organisation that operations with minimal ecological footprint and is committed to the sustainable development of Nigeria.
